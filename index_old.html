About Shinchan

ShinChan is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yoshito Usui. It follows the adventures of the five-year-old Shinnosuke "Shinchan" Nohara and his parents, baby sister, dog, neighbours, and friends and is set in Kasukabe, Saitama Prefecture.

Shinchan's characteristics

  1. He is super cute.
  2. He is quite naughty.
  3. He likes to sing.
